BULK Tile

The BULK Tile is where all the magic happens. It's a high-performance execution environment that runs entirely in memory on every validator node. This is the component that actually matches orders, manages risk, and handles liquidations—all at speeds measured in milliseconds.

What the Tile Contains

Matching Engine

The core trading logic. When buy and sell orders arrive, the matching engine pairs them together based on price and time priority. This happens in milliseconds, just like on a centralized exchange.

Risk Kernels

Risk management systems that handle margin requirements. Before allowing a trade, the Tile checks if the trader has enough collateral. These calculations happen instantly in memory.

Liquidation Engine

Monitors positions and triggers liquidations when traders fall below margin requirements. This runs continuously, checking every position to ensure the system stays solvent.

Why In-Memory Execution?

Millisecond latency: RAM operations take nanoseconds. Disk operations take milliseconds. Blockchain operations take seconds. By staying in memory, the Tile operates at the speed professional traders demand.

No blockchain constraints: The Tile doesn't have to wait for block production, compete for blockspace, or pay gas fees for every operation. It can process thousands of trades per second without slowdown.

The tradeoff: In-memory execution is fast but not persistent. That's why BULK Db exists—to save everything the Tile produces to permanent storage.

The Key Insight

The Tile demonstrates that you don't need a blockchain to execute trades trustlessly. You need deterministic logic running identically across multiple independent machines. The blockchain's job is to verify those machines agreed—not to execute the trades itself.
